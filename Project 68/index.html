Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

Although our school is a Christian school, it doesn't only support the Christian students. We have facilities other than the Church in order for all students to pray if they want at breaks. We also have Holy Mass after school on Friday.



School Library

Was built in 1980

Our school library has a variety of genres such as fantasy, horror, non-fiction and classics. Even though our school is a highschool, we still have books that are for kids to books that are for old people. All kids have to do to get their books is go to a librarian and give them the book they want to borrow and their school id.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

Our school has many school music programs such as concerts, instrument playing competitions(both for bands and individual people) and instrumental lessons. Our school has teachers for most instruments to help music students have a wider variety of instruments to choose from. Students can choose whether they want to do the music program or not want to do it.
